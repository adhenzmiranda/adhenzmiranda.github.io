Lando Norris

Lando Norris is a Belgian-British racing driver who drives for McLaren in Formula One. He was born in Bristol, England on November 13, 1999. He began karting at the age of seven and went on winning several championships throughout his junior career. In 2017, he joined the McLaren Young Driver Programme and debuted in Formula One in 2019. In his Formula One career, he has six podiums and one pole position. As a hobby, he competes in esports and creates his own race gear. He is fluent in English and some Flemish Dutch.

Oscar Piastri

Oscar Piastri is an Australian racing driver who currently drives for McLaren in Formula One. On April 6, 2001, he was born in Melbourne, Victoria, Australia. He began racing with remote-controlled cars and karts before transitioning to single-seaters in 2016. In 2019, he won the Formula Renault Eurocup, in 2020 the FIA Formula 3 Championship, and in 2021 the Formula 2 Championship. He is the first Formula One driver born in the twenty-first century and one of the sport's most promising young drivers. In 2023, he made his Formula One debut with McLaren, scoring points in his first race, the Australian Grand Prix.
