TF58

Tanner Foust

Tanner Foust is an American racing driver, stunt driver, and television host who competes in various disciplines such as rally, drift, ice racing, time attack, hill climb and rallycross. He has won 10 X Games gold medals, four RallyCross championships, and three Formula Drift championships. He is also known for co-hosting the American version of Top Gear and performing stunts for Hollywood films. He was born in Denver, Colorado, in 1973 and grew up in Scotland, where he discovered rallying. He graduated from the University of Colorado with a degree in molecular biology before pursuing a racing career. He started in karting and Formula Europa Boxer before moving to the U.S. and Europe to race in various series. He currently drives for Subaru Motorsports USA in the Nitro Rallycross series and also drives for NEOM McLaren in Extreme E.

Emma Gilmour

Emma Gilmour is a rally racer and vocalist from New Zealand who has accomplished a lot in her career. She is now a competitor in Extreme E. She is the first female factory driver for McLaren XE, one of the championship's nine teams. She began her motorsport career as a co-driver for a fellow driver before transitioning to driving herself. She was the first New Zealand woman to win a stage in the New Zealand Rally Championship and the first Kiwi woman to compete in the World Rally Championship. She is also a skilled singer, pianist, actress, presenter, and songwriter with a significant social media following. She enjoys music, animals, fitness, and traveling.
