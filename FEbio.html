Jake Hughes

Jake Hughes is a British racing driver who currently drives for McLaren in Formula E. He was born in Birmingham, England, on May 30, 1994. He began racing in karting and Formula BMW before continuing to Formula 3 and Formula 2. In 2013, he won the first BRDC Formula 4 Championship, and he has numerous wins and podiums in various F3 championships. In 2020, he made his Formula 2 debut with HWA Racelab, finishing third in the Bahrain sprint event. For the 2022/23 Formula E season, he joined McLaren and won two pole positions in his first nine races.

René Rast

René Rast is a German racing driver who currently drives for McLaren in Formula E. He was born in Minden, West Germany, on October 26, 1986. He began his racing career in karting and then moved to sports car racing. He has won numerous major titles and races in GT and endurance categories, including three DTM championships with Audi (2017, 2019 and 2020). He also competed in the FIA World Endurance Championship in the Le Mans 24 Hours. He debuted in Formula E with Team Aguri in 2016 and joined Audi Sport Abt Schaeffler in 2019. He achieved three Formula E podiums before joining McLaren in 2023.
